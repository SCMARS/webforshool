Gleb Ukhovskyi

I am a Ukrainian champion in the OK Senior category, a WORLD Cup finalist, and have stood on the podium at the Euro Series in Romania in 2023 in Rotax Senior.

My Story

I started karting at the age of 7 and progressed to the OKJ category, where I felt very comfortable. I became the Ukrainian champion in the OKJ class. In late 2022, I participated in my first international race at the ROK Cup Festival in Italy. I'll never forget the feeling of racing on Italian soil for the first time.
